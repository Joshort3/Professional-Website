Community Service

Little Elm Food Bank
 - Volunteered at the Little Food to unpackage and organize food into categories. Also aided in repackaging food to be shipped. The Little Elm Food Bank provides food for children, working poor, and seniors.

Little Elm Road Cleanup
 - Picked up trash near high school roads. The high school contains over thousand students, so the roads and grass are a big source of pollution. Monthly cleaning was highly needed.
Johnny Broyle's Nature Trail Cleanup
 - Aided by picking up trash on the lake trail. Jonny Broyle's Nature Trail is widely used by Little Elm community for running and bike riding. Also has spots by the lake for fishing. Cleaning up the trail allows for nature to be truely preserved, so people have a place to breath fresh air and relax.
